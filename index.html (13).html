How It Works

Three steps. We do the heavy lifting.

Step 01

You Choose

Pick the gift category, the treats, size, and quantity for your whole list — then send us your client list with names and mailing addresses.

Step 02

We Coordinate

We set up your account, build the branded gift card and note card with your logo and message, send you a proof, then place the full order.

Step 03

They Enjoy

Sugarwish packs your selections and ships to every client's door in their signature gift box — with your branding and message inside. Zero follow-up for you.
