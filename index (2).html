How It Works

Three steps. We do the heavy lifting.

Step 01

You Pick

Choose a gift category, size, and quantity below — then send us your recipient list. Names and emails are all we need.

Step 02

We Coordinate

We set up your account, build the branded gift card and note card with your logo and message, send you a proof, then deliver to every recipient by email or text.

Step 03

They Choose

Each recipient opens their gift, picks their own favorites, and enters their own delivery address. Sugarwish packs and ships free — zero follow-up for you.
